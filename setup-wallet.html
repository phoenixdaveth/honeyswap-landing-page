Setup MetaMask Wallet

Metamask is our current wallet of choice for using the xDai network. It is the most universal wallet currently for web3. This tutorial will guide you through the process of installing and setting up the Metamask wallet for the xDai network.

Step 1: Head to https://metamask.io/download.html and download MetaMask for the device you want to install it on (we recommend using it on windows with chrome) & proceed through the installation including backing up your new wallet or importing an existing wallet.

Step 3: Go to your Metamask account and click on the ‘Settings’ option.

Step 4:Choose ‘Networks’ as shown.

Step 5:Click on ‘Add Network’ as shown.

Step 6:Enter the following information for xDai chain as shown below and click 'Save'.

Network Name: xDai
New RPC URL: https://rpc.xdaichain.com/
Chain ID: 0x64
Symbol: xDai
Block Explorer URL: https://blockscout.com/poa/xdai

Alternative ‘New RPC URL’s:
https://dai.poa.network/
https://xdai.poanetwork.dev/
https://xdai.1hive.org/
Alternative Chain ID: 100

Step 7:Now just choose the xDai network you created and your Metamask wallet is ready to function on Honeyswap and on the xDai network.

Congratulations! Your MetaMask wallet setup is now completed. If you need further help with setting up Metamask or troubleshooting, please visit our help section in the forums or discord.
